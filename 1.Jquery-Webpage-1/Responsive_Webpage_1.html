NEW WORLD SOPHISTICATION



The Deer Path Inn guest rooms and suites have set a new standard for comfort on Chicago’s North Shore. Not simply redecorated, our new guest rooms and suites have been entirely re-envisioned to introduce modern-day luxury and amenities. Ranging from 350 to 800 sq. ft., these graciously appointed accommodations feature furnishings, fabrics and artwork that suggest an appreciation for timelessly curated interiors.

NOBLE KING ROOMS

Original artwork, fine upholstered furnishings, and floor-to- ceiling drapery add fresh, modern sophistication to our Noble King Rooms, which feature all-stone bathrooms and luxurious Frette linens.

NOBLE DOUBLE QUEEN ROOMS

From original artwork and floor-to-ceiling drapery to all-stone bathrooms and Frette linens, every fresh, modern décor detail in our Noble Queen Rooms is designed to offer you truly serene comfort.

MANOR HOUSE KING SUITE

Even if you weren’t ‘to the manor born’ the grace and comfort of these generously appointed suites, will soon have you feeling as if you were. Combining a range of sophisticated modern amenities with the comfort and elegance of years gone by, our Manor House Suites truly offer you the very best of both worlds.

MANOR HOUSE KING SUITE WITH WET BAR

With their gracious floor plans, there is just something about these stylish suites that make you never want to leave. And with their cleverly appealing wet bars, you may never have to. So whether you decide to eat-in or to enjoy the epicurean delights of our English Room or White Hart Pub, the delightful comforts of these suites will keep you coming back for more.

ROYAL KING SUITE

The epitome of luxurious comfort, the Royal Suite is a charming and graceful retreat featuring a comfortable king bed finished with Frette linens and a complimentary fully stocked bar with premium wines, spirits and English-inspired treats. All have beautifully furnished sitting areas, including many with fireplaces.

CORNWALL SUITE

With its gently glowing fireplace and exquisite blend of handsome furnishings and thoughtful amenities, our Cornwall Suite will soon leave you feeling like the honored guest of a weekend party at an elegant English country estate. Highlights include an all-stone bathroom with glass enclosed shower and freestanding tub that fills from a cascading ceiling fixture.

BUCKINGHAM SUITE

Palatial in size, the elegant Buckingham Suite is ideal for those who seek the ultimate in comfort and style. Featuring an all-stone bathroom with a glass-enclosed shower and freestanding tub that fills from a cascading ceiling fixture, every aspect of this one-of-kind suite is truly stunning.

NOBLE KING ACCESSIBLE

Original artwork, fine upholstered furnishings, and floor-to-ceiling drapery add fresh, modern sophistication to our Noble King Rooms, which feature all-stone bathrooms and luxurious Frette linens. This room also provides several accessible features (see 'Accessibility Features') to better suit your needs.
